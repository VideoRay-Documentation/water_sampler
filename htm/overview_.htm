Water Sampler Overview

The Water Sampler consists of a remotely operated syringe that can be used to collect water samples. It can be easily mounted on the ROV and can be used for other purposes such as to expel fluids such as dies for leak detection.

VideoRay Water Sampler mounted on a Pro 4

The Water Sampler can be used with almost all other VideoRay accessories, except for the manipulator or cutter.
