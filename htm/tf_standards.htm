Standards Applied

The following standards have been applied and adhered to in the design, production and testing of the Water Sampler.

  1. Health and Safety at Work Act 1974
  2. Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999
  3. Workplace (Health, Safety and Welfare) REgulations 1992 (As amended 2002)
  4. SSP1.70 (CDM) Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2007
  5. Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC
  6. Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (as amended 2002)
  7. Supply of Machinery (Safety) regulations 2008
  8. Health and (Safety Signs and Signals) Regulations (as amended 2007)
  9. Lifting Operations and Lifting Equipment Regulations 1998 (LOLER)
  10. Provision and Use of Work Equipment Regulations (1998 (PUWER)
  11. BS EN ISO 12100:2010 Safety of Machinery. General principles for design. Risk Assessment and risk reduction.
  12. BS EN 12100-2-2003 Safety of Machinery. Basic concepts, general principles for design. Technical Principles, Basic Terminology, methodology.
  13. The Electricity at work regulations 1989 no. 635 (maintained by HSE in the UK)
  14. BS 7671:2008 Requirements for electrical installations 17th Edition
  15. EMC directive 2004/108/EC
  16. BS EN 60204-1:2006 Electrical safety of machines - General requirements
  17. COSHH Regulations: 2002 (as amended 2003 and 2004)
  18. Noise at Work Regulations: 2005 (as amended)
