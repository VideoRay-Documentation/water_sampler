Accessory Compatibility

The Water Sampler is compatible with most accessories. Mounting is the only limitation.

Communications

The Water Sampler uses the 9 Pin accessory port on the back of the ROV.

Pro 4

Function Pins Use
Video 1, 2 No
ROV Communications Bus 7, 8 Yes
12 V DC Power 9, 3 Yes

Spare Pair 4, 6 No

Function Pins Use
Video 1, 2 No
Manipulator Power Bus 7, 8 Yes

Mounting

The Water Sampler can be mounted on the normal skid mount or on the sonar mount if the sonar is attached.
